About

Image from Sie7eparrafos The Montserrat font was created by Julieta Ulanovsky. She developed the font in 2010, but didn't publicly release it until 2011. She graduated from the University of Buenos Aires (Uba) and became a typeface designer. Montserrat is inspired by the same name of the neighborhood in Buenos Aires, Argentina. The font is widely used for signage creating and urban typohraphy to Buenos Aires. This font is still available to this day and has been used throughout advertising including websites and branding. It is a serif type font that is similar to those of Arial and Helvetica
